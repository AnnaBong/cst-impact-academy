04 · FULL COURSE CURRICULUM

What participants learn,
module by module.

Each module builds on the last, creating a cumulative system of thinking tools that participants can apply immediately in their work context.

MODULE 01 · BOTH FULL COURSES

3A+P Framework —
The Articulation Engine

Before solving a problem, we must articulate it precisely. 3A+P provides a structured way to construct a situation, conversation or prompt.

  • Approach — how you enter a problem or conversation
  • Aspect — the dimensions you choose to observe
  • Angle — the specific perspective you take
  • Presupposition — assumptions shaping your view
  • Apply 3A+P to workplace communication and AI prompts
CORE FRAMEWORK
MODULE 02 · BOTH FULL COURSES

Structural Thinking —
See the Whole System

Move beyond managing symptoms. Learn to diagnose organisations, teams and challenges as living, dynamic systems.

  • Elements, interconnections and goals
  • Stocks, flows and feedback loops
  • Identify recurring system archetypes
  • Map a team or business unit as a system
  • Understand resilience and efficiency trade-offs
MEADOWS’ FRAMEWORK
MODULE 03 · BOTH FULL COURSES

12 Leverage Points —
Where Power Lives in a System

Learn where high-impact change actually lives, from visible structures and information flows to goals, mindsets and paradigms.

  • Structural layer: numbers, buffers and physical structure
  • Feedback layer: information flows, loop strength and delays
  • Paradigm layer: goals, mindsets and assumptions
  • Map current interventions on the leverage scale
  • Identify the highest-leverage change available
